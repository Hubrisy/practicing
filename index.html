Welcome to DjookyX

DjookyX is the world’s first music investment and
trading online platform, a marketplace where artists
can sell intellectual property (IP) rights in their music to
fans and investors who, in return, will benefit jointly from
the potential future income generated by the song(s)
they support.
How does it work
Artists
  1. Upload your song(s) to the marketplace
  2. Decide how many IP rights you would like to sell
  3. Set the minimum price per IP right
  4. Set up a start date for the count down
  5. Agree to the T&Cs for the initial sale and transfer
    of the song rights
Investors
  1. Buy IP rights in the song of your favourite artist
  2. Sell at any time and transfer your IP rights to other investors if
    you decide to do so
  3. Enjoy high security of transactions and liquidity of your assets
Why should i sign up?
DjookyX for Artists
  1. Access the global investor community
  2. Generate the funds needed to develop and accelerate your
    music career
  3. Retain your freedom while sharing the royalties with the
    community of music fans who have supported you on your
    way to success
DjookyX for Investors
  1. For the first time in history you have the opportunity to
    invest and trade in music as a new and exciting asset class
  2. Together with the Artists you will benefit from the
    royalties a song may be generating in the future
  3. Become an ethical investor supporting the new
    generation of creators from across the world
Security
We use sophisticated technology and embedded procedures to ensure the highest security levels for every transaction.

Our public register of the owners of music rights is supported by the blockchain technology ensuring legal compliance as well as accuracy,
transparency, and real-time record of rights ownership.
